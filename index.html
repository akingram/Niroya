About NIROYA

Excellent Logistics and Supply Chain Management

NIROYA is a leading trading company based in Nigeria that specializes in the exportation of high-quality cocoa beans and cashew. NIROYA sources its products directly from farmers, ensuring that the cocoa beans and cashew nuts are of the highest quality, and are ethically and sustainably sourced.

Since 2025 NIROYA has sourced and exported more than 150,000Mt. of cocoa beans and raw cashew nuts from Nigeria and has proven a reliable supplier of high-quality products to the markets.

Our Products

Premium Quality Agricultural Products

COCOA

Cocoa is a tropical evergreen tree that produces cocoa beans, which are used to make chocolate. NIROYA sources its beans directly from farmers in the region, who produce some of the finest cocoa in the country. NIROYA is dedicated to providing farmers with necessary resources and training to increase yield and quality of the beans.

CASHEW

NIROYA is a leading exporter of premium quality cashew nuts, sourcing from over 19 states across Nigeria, each renowned for producing exceptional cashews. Committed to sustainable practices, NIROYA actively empowers farmers with the resources and knowledge they need to optimize their cashew harvests, fostering a thriving and resilient cashew industry.
