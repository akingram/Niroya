About SGC

Excellent Logistics and Supply Chain Management

Sunbeth Global Concepts (SGC) is a leading trading company based in Nigeria that specializes in the exportation of high-quality cocoa beans and cashew. SGC sources its products directly from farmers, ensuring that the cocoa beans and cashew nuts are of the highest quality, and are ethically and sustainably sourced.

Since 2017 Sunbeth has sourced and exported more than 150,000Mt. of cocoa beans and raw cashew nuts from Nigeria and has proven a reliable supplier of high-quality products to the markets.

SGC is Rainforest Alliance Certified on Cocoa Bean

The Rainforest Alliance certification is a well-respected and globally recognized label that is awarded to companies that meet strict standards of sustainability, social responsibility, and environmental conservation. This significant milestone is a testament to Sunbeths commitment to sustainability, transparency, and the well-being of its farmers and the environment.

Our Products

COCOA

Cocoa is a tropical evergreen tree that produces cocoa beans, which are used to make chocolate. SGC sources its beans directly from farmers in the region, who produce some of the finest cocoa in the country. SGC is dedicated to providing farmers with necessary resources and training to increase yield and quality of the beans.

CASHEW

Sunbeth Global Concepts (SGC) is a leading exporter of premium quality cashew nuts, sourcing from over 19 states across Nigeria, each renowned for producing exceptional cashews. Committed to sustainable practices, SGC actively empowers farmers with the resources and knowledge they need to optimize their cashew harvests, fostering a thriving and resilient cashew industry.

Sustainability

We are committed to best practices!

In addition to checking the quality of the cocoa beans, SGC also conducts regular audits of its suppliers and farmers to ensure that they are following ethical and sustainable practices. This includes checking for proper labor practices, use of pesticides, and other factors that can impact the quality of the cocoa beans.
