About Our Company

Our Story

Founded in 1990, NIROYA began as a small trading firm with a vision to connect commodity producers with international markets. Over the past three decades, we have grown into a global leader in commodity trading and supply chain solutions.

Our success is built on long-term relationships, market expertise, and a commitment to ethical business practices. We pride ourselves on delivering value to all stakeholders while maintaining the highest standards of integrity.

Our Values
