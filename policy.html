Policy & Compliance

Our commitment to ethical business practices and regulatory compliance

Compliance Framework

At NIROYA, we maintain a robust compliance framework that ensures we operate within all applicable laws, regulations, and industry standards across all jurisdictions where we conduct business.

Ethical Business Practices

We are committed to conducting business with the highest ethical standards. Our Code of Conduct outlines the principles that guide our operations and relationships:

  • Zero tolerance for bribery and corruption
  • Fair competition and antitrust compliance
  • Conflicts of interest avoidance
  • Accurate record-keeping and reporting
  • Protection of confidential information

Anti-Money Laundering (AML)

We have implemented comprehensive AML policies and procedures to detect and prevent money laundering activities:

  • Customer due diligence and Know Your Customer (KYC) procedures
  • Transaction monitoring and reporting
  • Employee training programs
  • Regular independent audits

Sanctions Compliance

We strictly adhere to all international sanctions regimes and have implemented systems to ensure compliance:

  • Regular screening of clients and partners against sanctions lists
  • Geographic restrictions on certain transactions
  • Ongoing monitoring of regulatory changes

Environmental Policy

We are committed to minimizing our environmental impact and promoting sustainable practices:

  • Compliance with environmental regulations
  • Sustainable sourcing initiatives
  • Carbon footprint reduction programs
  • Support for environmental conservation projects

Health and Safety

The health and safety of our employees, partners, and communities is our priority:

  • Comprehensive safety protocols for all operations
  • Regular safety training and drills
  • Emergency response planning
  • Continuous improvement of safety standards

Reporting Concerns

We encourage employees and stakeholders to report any concerns about potential violations of our policies or applicable laws through our confidential reporting channels:

  • Email: compliance@niroya.com
  • Hotline: +1 (555) 999-REPORT
  • Online reporting portal
